# FOUNDER LOAD REDUCTION

## Objective

Reduce founder operational load by 30% within 6 months.

---

## Client Request Intake

All new requests must go through structured intake form.

Tag as:
- Included
- Change Order
- New Proposal

No informal commitments.

---

## Proposal Standardization

Modular template with:
- Scope
- Tier Options
- Investment Summary
- Terms

Goal: < 30 minute turnaround.

---

## Production Playbook

Must include:
- Pre-production checklist
- Crew brief template
- Load-in timeline
- Show-day checklist
- Strike checklist
- Post-event margin review

Founder reviews — does not execute.

---

## Strategy Discipline

Strategy review only once per month.
